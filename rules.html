Frequently Asked Questions

Is this considered gambling?
No. GoldenGuess is a "Sales Promotion" under the New Zealand Gambling Act 2003. No purchase is necessary to enter, and no consideration (money) is paid by the participant. It is a free-to-play competition promoting Harcourts Golden Links listings.
What if multiple people guess the exact price?
If multiple participants predict the exact hammer price (down to the dollar), the jackpot prize pool will be split equally among the winners.
When do I get paid?
Winners will be contacted via their verified mobile number within 24 hours of the auction. Prizes are transferred via bank deposit within 5 working days after identity verification.
